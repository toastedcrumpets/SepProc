Past and Future Exams

Current Exam information

  • The current exam format is a 3-hour in-person invigilated exam.
  • Five questions must be answered.
  • The exam format has changed over the years, the 2019 paper is representative of the current format.
  • Examinations in 2020, 2021, and 2022 were online tests with automatically generated varations. A version of this is available for your own practice on the MyAberdeen site, but is not representative of the in-person exam.
